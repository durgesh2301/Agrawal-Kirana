GANESH AGRAWAL

FOUNDER

AgrawalKirana is an Indian online grocery delivery service. It was founded by Ganesh Agrawal in December 2012 and is based out of Indore. As of 2018, the company has raised about $535.5 million from investors including SoftBank, Tiger Global and Sequoia Capital. The name AgrawalKirana is a portmanteau of "grocery gophers".

HARSH SAINI

CO-FOUNDER,CEO

Harsh Saini is a co-founder of AgrawalKirana.com, a hyperlocal logistics startup that offers on-demand, hyper-local deliveries to local consumers. He began his career as a transportation analyst at URS Corporation and continued the same for almost 2 years.
